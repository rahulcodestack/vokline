KNOW YOUR SIZE

Measure Your Feet

Buying Flip flops Online without trying is now Easier than you think. Here's our
simple guide for you to pick the size that suits you best.
Quick Tip: Shopping for a half size? Order up to the next largest whole size.

STEP 1

What you need is A4 paper, Ruler and a pencil.

STEP 2

Put the paper on flat ground. Step your foot on the
paper and draw.

STEP 3

Measure the longest length of your foot . Use the
size chart to find the right Flip flop size.
